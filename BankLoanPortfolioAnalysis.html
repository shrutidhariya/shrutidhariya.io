A Comprehensive Bank Loan Summary Dashboard

    This interactive Tableau dashboard offers a detailed 360-degree overview of bank loan portfolios. It comprises of 3 dashboards, including key loan metrics as:
  • Good vs Bad Loans: Visualize the distribution of performing and non-performing loans.
  • Loan Details: Drill down into loan specifics, including amount, interest rate, and term
  • State-Level Analysis: Monitor loan trends and concentrations by state
  • Monthly Insights: Track loan performance and origination trends over time
  • Loan Status: Monitor the status of loans, such as active, paid, and defaulted
  • Loan Purpose: Analyze loan purposes, such as debt consolidation, personal loans
